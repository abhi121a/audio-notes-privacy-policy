Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2, 2026

1. Introduction

Audio Notes is designed to help you create, save, edit, and share speech-based notes. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app uses, how it is used, and what choices you have.

2. Information We Collect

Microphone Access

Audio Notes requests microphone access to support speech-to-text note creation.

  • Audio is used only while you actively record.
  • We do not intentionally store raw microphone audio in the app.
  • Speech recognition may use on-device or network-based recognition services provided by the device or platform.

Notes and App Content

  • The text notes you create are stored locally on your device.
  • Note content is not uploaded by the app to our own servers.
  • If you choose to share a note, the content is sent only through the app or service you select from the system share sheet.

Local App Preferences

  • The app stores certain settings locally on your device, such as privacy notice acceptance and theme/background preference.

Firebase Analytics

Audio Notes uses Google Firebase Analytics to understand app usage and improve the app. Firebase Analytics may collect app usage and device-related information such as event timestamps, app version, device/OS information, screen or activity information, app instance identifiers, and similar analytics data associated with your use of the app.

In addition to automatically collected analytics events, Audio Notes logs custom analytics events for:

  • log_mic_pressed
  • log_note_saved
  • log_note_shared

These analytics events are used to measure feature usage. We do not intentionally include note text, microphone audio, or full note contents in these analytics event names or parameters.

3. How We Use Information

  • Convert speech into text notes.
  • Display, edit, save, and delete notes on your device.
  • Allow you to share notes when you choose to do so.
  • Understand feature usage and improve app reliability and usability through analytics.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data.

  • Notes remain stored locally unless you intentionally share them.
  • Analytics data is processed by Google Firebase / Google Analytics as part of the analytics service used by the app.
  • If you share a note using another app, that app’s own privacy policy and handling rules apply.

5. Permissions and Network Use

Microphone (RECORD_AUDIO)

Used for speech-to-text note creation.

Internet

Internet access may be used for speech recognition services, Firebase Analytics event delivery, and related Firebase functionality.

6. Data Storage and Security

  • Your notes are stored locally on your device.
  • The app does not use its own cloud backend to store your notes.
  • We take reasonable steps to limit data collection to what is needed for app functionality and analytics.

7. Your Choices

  • You can deny microphone permission, although this will limit core speech-to-text features.
  • You can delete notes at any time within the app.
  • You can uninstall the app to remove locally stored app data from your device.
  • You can stop using the app if you do not want analytics-enabled usage data to be generated.

8. Children’s Privacy

Audio Notes is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected by changing the “Last updated” date on this page.
