Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 29, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Audio Notes.

This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use the application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency in how data is used.

2. Information We Collect

Microphone Access

Audio Notes requires access to your device’s microphone to:

  • Record your voice input
  • Convert speech into text notes

Important:

  • Audio is used only during active recording
  • Audio is not stored permanently by the app

Notes Data

  • The app stores text notes generated from your speech
  • These notes are stored locally on your device only

We do NOT upload your notes to any external server.

3. How We Use Information

We use the collected data solely to:

  • Convert speech to text
  • Display and save your notes
  • Enable sharing of notes via other apps (e.g., email, messaging)

4. Data Sharing

We do not:

  • Sell your data
  • Share your data with third parties
  • Store your data on external servers

5. Permissions Used

Microphone (RECORD_AUDIO)

Used to capture audio for speech-to-text functionality.

Internet (if applicable)

Used only if speech recognition services require network access.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your data:

  • Notes are stored locally on your device
  • No external database or cloud storage is used by the app

7. Your Control

  • You can delete notes anytime within the app
  • You can uninstall the app to remove all stored data
  • You can deny microphone permission (this will disable core functionality)

8. Children’s Privacy

Audio Notes does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with an updated “Last updated” date.
